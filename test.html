Neondish Lean Canvas

Problem

Founders of startups require lots of things to start, manage, and grow their company — Investors, Accelerators, Workspace, Lawyers, Accountants, etc. — yet there is no comprehensive resource for them to search and find these things.

Solution

A web-based platform where entrepreneurs can go to find the resources, investors, and accelerators to start, run, and grow their business.

Our core product matches companies with the accelerators and investors who are most likely to invest in their company, based on their thesis and historical investment data.

Key Metrics

Amount of web traffic.

Amount of user sign-ups.

Amount of active users.

Amount of emails on mailing list.

Unique Value Proposition

We save entrepreneurs the many hours of time spent searching for investors, accelerators, and resources by consolidating all of them into one comprehensive, easy to use platform.
